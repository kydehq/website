AI Agents Are Being Deployed Across Every Regulated Industry. The Audit Trail Requirements Are the Same Everywhere.

KYDE WITNESS provides tamper-evident governance for any organization deploying AI agents — regardless of sector, provider, or use case.

The EU AI Act, NIS-2, DORA, and GDPR do not care which LLM vendor you chose. They care about what your AI systems did, whether you can prove it, and whether the record can be trusted.

KYDE WITNESS answers all three — in five minutes.

01

HR Technology & Recruiting

EU AI Act — High Risk

The Challenge

AI agents in HR are classified as High-Risk under the EU AI Act (Annex III). Automated screening, scoring, and shortlisting of candidates are subject to mandatory logging, human oversight requirements, and audit-readiness obligations — with enforcement beginning August 2, 2026.

What KYDE WITNESS Does

Every candidate interaction, every automated scoring decision, and every tool call made by your recruiting AI is captured in a tamper-evident append-only database. When a rejected candidate challenges the decision, you have a complete, unalterable record: what the model saw, what it decided, and why.

Highest EU AI Act Risk Class

AI in healthcare — clinical decision support, diagnostic tools, patient data processing — carries the highest EU AI Act risk classification. The evidence requirements are strict, the liability exposure is severe, and 'we can't access the logs' is not an acceptable answer to a regulator, auditor, or court.
