KYDE Labs

Where We Push the Boundary of What Governed AI Agents Can Do.

The Lab

KYDE Labs explores what becomes possible when AI agents operate with verifiable trust — in the physical world, at scale.

KYDE WITNESS solves the governance problem for AI agents in enterprise software. Labs asks the next question: what happens when those same agents step outside the browser and into physical systems?

Energy grids. Industrial infrastructure. Autonomous hardware. Environments where a bad decision doesn't produce a wrong document — it trips a breaker, misallocates storage capacity, or causes a grid imbalance at 2am.

The audit requirements are higher. The stakes are higher. The governance layer has to be deeper.

Labs — Physical Infrastructure

First Published Use Case

AI agent governance for energy infrastructure and battery management systems.

Energy Infrastructure

AI agents managing load balancing, grid dispatch, and operational decisions — with every action signed, timestamped, and verifiable.

Battery Management

Automated charge/discharge decisions in large-scale battery storage — governed by the same tamper-evident append-only database that runs in enterprise software stacks.
